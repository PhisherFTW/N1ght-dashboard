Demo 1 Faceit Mirage (team_iStunzy 17-19 team_gashdog)

Focus areas:

  1. Don’t get distracted by teammates’ calls causing you not to follow through with the correct protocols such as clearing angles or dropping useful utility or taking care when rotating.
  2. Getting kill hungry and going to kills that don’t actively benefit the round and just are there to pad your score but could potentially cause you to have a weak buy the following round or turn the round into the enemy's favour.
  3. When peeking an angle never jump into an angle as you are inaccurate for a time after you land and are unable to shoot and thus causing a delay in TTD (time to damage) which is never good.
  4. Impatient when holding good angles for the duration of the game you hardly ever held a useful angle longer than 10 seconds because you were constantly switching.
  5. Crouching to stop instead of using counter-strafing which causes you to shoot later and completely removes your movement from the engagement making it much easier for the enemy to predict exactly where you head will be.
  6. Throwing away your positional advantage for a marginally better position which left you completely out in the open.
  7. Do not hold fading smokes as the enemy in the smoke will always have a big advantage.
  8. Don’t get used to not using cover because although off-angle are good if there is a second to trade your first kill without cover is drastically decreases your chance of getting the second kill.
  9. Do not overcommit to fights and expose yourself to multiple enemies this drastically decreases your chances of getting kills and allows them a higher chance of instantly killing you.
  10. Make sure your crosshair is locked onto the enemy before you start shooting, a few times I noticed you started shooting before your crosshair was not on the enemy before you started shooting.
  11. When taking engagements you take the first one that pops into your mind this isn’t nessecarily bad but it makes a lot of your peeks extremely predictable and that is why during quite a few of your engagements it feels like you can barely react before the enemies kill you
  12. Practise Multi-strafing as in a few situations in would have been very beneficial for you and could have prevented you dying as well as increased the number of kill you got. youtube:
